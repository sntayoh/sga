About Me

A senior economist with a substantial background in preparing and producing annual and strategic plans. Experienced in creating monitoring and evaluation toolkits and manuals. Capable to design and produce various programs and/or project reports. Design and undertake monitoring and evaluation missions to project sites. Skilled at data analysis and interpretation via MIS and creating reports with visualization and dashboards. I am looking for work as a senior economist, monitoring and evaluation consultant, and project coordinator to advance my management career.
I have more than 15 years of experience as an Economist and I am adept in planning, reporting, monitoring, and data analysis. Aside from the fact that my work experience has given me a diverse skill set, including excellent project management and preparing documentation skills, I excel at the following:
 Designing robust monitoring and evaluation plan with the theory of change and logical framework.
 Prepare, produce and make plans and reports of programs/projects using Power BI with dashboards
 Supervise programs and/or project tracks through Microsoft project planning and other tools
 Create and apply virtual questionnaires via Kobo Toolbox and analyze them with STATA and SPSS
I have been working as a senior Economist in WRDF/MoWE for six years, and have gained a solid grasp of creating how to plan, report, and design monitoring and evaluating manuals and toolkits. Recently, I signed a contract to design and submit an M & E plan document with the International Fund for Agricultural Development (IFAD) for the IGREENFIN program that I have delivered.
In my prior positions, I was responsible for collecting, organizing, and analyzing data for planning, monitoring, and reporting in the Ministry of Water and Energy's OWNP Program, which was funded by the World Bank, DFID, and UNICEF. Additionally, I have delivered various toolkits and manuals to follow up and evaluate projects in governmental and non-governmental organizations.
In addition to my experience and personal qualities, I have a Master’s degree in Economics and a passion for extracting clarity in applied economics. I am excited about the opportunity to join your organization/institution and add my experience to your team.
